Walking Holidays in Scotland

As one of the leading walking holiday tour operators in Scotland, we offer you an unrivalled walking and hiking experience through the spectacular scenery of the Scottish Highlands.

Join a small group of like-minded travellers and explore Scotland with the experts.

All our walking holidays in Scotland are graded to allow you to choose from a relaxed and leisurely paced walking holiday to a strenuous and challenging trip of a lifetime.

Select from our easier walking holidays, weekends, day hiking tours or our more challenging hiking trips.

All our week-long walking holidays are fully inclusive - including all meals, accommodation, guiding and transport from Edinburgh.

Our many years of experience and great love of our country will enable us to guide you through the most beautiful areas in Scotland, offering you a unique insight into its history, culture and breathtaking scenery.

If you would like us to organise a bespoke walking tour for your group - contact us for more information - we also cater for corporate groups and outdoor teambuilding events and can advise on all aspects of walking and hiking in Scotland.
